background-color with HSLA

H-->Hue-- degree on the color wheel(0 to 360) 0 or 360 is red

S--> saturation--> 100% is the full color

L-->Lightness--> 0% is black and 100% is white

A--> alpha value--> opacity

background-color: hsla(240, 50%, 70%, 0.3);
